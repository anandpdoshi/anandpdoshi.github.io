IEEE Brainwaves is the Student Chapter (code: 60401) of the IEEE in Dwarkadas J. Sanghvi College of Engineering , one of the premier engineering colleges in Mumbai. It is duly recognized by the IEEE, USA.

MEMBERSHIP PROMOTION
The goodwill of IEEE Brainwaves and the opportunities it provides to neophytes, attracted 150 new members.
These include the 1st, 2nd & 3rd year students from the six engineering departments of :
1. Electronics Enginering
2. Electronics & Telecommunication Engineering
3. Information Technology
4. Computer Engineering
5. Biomedical Engineering
6. Production Engineering

EDUCATION
The event of Tehcnical Paper Presentation in Techtrix '07 gave new insights into the latest or upcoming technological theories in the fields of Communication, Biomedical Engineering, Electronics & Computers.
Robokriti : the robotics workshop covered assembling a wired / wireless robot, learning basic concepts of Electronics and Robotics (for convenience of freshers) and an introduction to aquabotics. The importance of Robocon : The International level Robotics competition was also highlighted in the workshop. At the end of the workshop, a CD containing important information discussed in the workshop, was provided to each team for future reference.

PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT
In organizing Techtrix '07, the members developed basic managerial skills. The sponsorship team presented the festival's potential to prospective sponsors. Such presentation skills are a key factor in the professional life.

COMMUNITY SERVICE
PETA (People for Ethical Treatment of Animals), world's largest animal rights organization, was invited to Techtrix '07 to create awareness about the sufferings of animals & attract potential members.

SOCIAL ACTIVITIES
Techtrix '07, an inter collegiate festival was held on 21st, 22nd & 23rd September which consisted of Original, innovative and intellectually stimulating events, coupled with fun and intelligent contests. Students representing various colleges across Mumbai participated in the events.

COMMUNICATION
Members are kept informed of the upcoming events through this website & SMS services. Also a desk is setup atleast a week before any upcoming to advertise the event to the members & non members alike.

FUTURE PLANNING
The present committee plans to increase the initial fund which the future committee will receive so that they can embark on bigger challanges.
