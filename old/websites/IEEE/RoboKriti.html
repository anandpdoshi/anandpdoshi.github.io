IEEE Brainwaves DJSCoE and Technophilia Solutions organized "Robokriti", the robotics workshop on the 1st and 2nd of September, 2007.

The two day workshop was conducted by Hiral Sanghvi (Technophilia Solutions), who conducts many robotics workshops at national level.

It covered assembling a wired / wireless robot, learning basic concepts of Electronics and Robotics (for convenience of freshers) and an introduction to aquabotics. The importance of Robocon : The International level Robotics competition was also highlighted in the workshop. At the end of the workshop, a CD containing important information discussed in the workshop, was provided to each team for future reference.
